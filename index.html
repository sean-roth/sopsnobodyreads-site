How it works

From binder to verified training in weeks, not months.

01

Assessment

We review your SOPs, interview key personnel, and map your documentation against regulatory requirements. You get a prioritized action plan and a sample module built from one of your actual procedures.

02

Module production

Each SOP becomes a narrative training module with comprehension checks. Built around your procedures, your equipment, your chemicals. Not generic safety clipart and bullet points.

03

Verified delivery

Your team takes the training, signs off with a PIN, and completes the quiz. Every completion generates a timestamped, hashed record. Your managers see who's trained, who's not, and can prove it to any auditor.

The audit trail

Training records that can't be faked.

Every course completion is cryptographically hashed and anchored to the Bitcoin network via OpenTimestamps. The record is tamper-proof and independently verifiable. No one can alter a completion after the fact, and no one can fabricate one that didn't happen.

When an inspector asks for proof of training, you hand them a PDF with a verifiable hash. Not a binder. Not a spreadsheet. A mathematically provable record.
